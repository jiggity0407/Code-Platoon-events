Welcome to Ube treats!

This site provides you with ube recipe ideas & locations to shop for products..

Ube has a nutty sweet potato like flavor and is usually purple.
Because of its sweet taste and bright color it is often used in
desserts and baking. The best place to buy local ube products
is at your local filipino market. Because ube can be hard to buy
fresh, powders and extracts are available for cooking.
Ube treats exist in the form of ice cream, cookies,
cupcakes and even donuts.
