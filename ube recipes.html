    Welcome to Ube treats!

    This site provides you with ube recipe ideas & locations to shop
    Ube is a purple yam that orgininated in southeast asia, grown throughout the phillipines and said to have originated from New Guinea via trade. Ube has a nutty sweet potato like flavor and is usually purple. Because of its sweet taste and bright color it is often used in desserts and baking. The best place to buy local ube products is at your local filipino market. Because ube can be hard to buy fresh, powders and extracts are available for Cooking. Ube treats exist in the form of ice cream, cookies, cupcakes and even donuts.
